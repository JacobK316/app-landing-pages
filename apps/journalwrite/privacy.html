Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

JournalWrite is designed to be simple and private. Your data stays on your device, and we do not collect or track your personal information.

Your Data

All journal entries and content you create are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to external servers.

No Data Collection

JournalWrite does not use accounts, analytics, or tracking. No personal information is collected or stored by the app.

Sharing

You may choose to share your entries using the app's built-in sharing features. Once shared, that content is handled by the app or service you choose.

Support

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address only to respond to your message. We do not use it for tracking or marketing.

Security

Your data is protected by your device’s security (such as passcode or biometrics). You are responsible for backing up your data, as we cannot recover lost entries.

Your Control

You have full control over your data. You can edit or delete your journal entries at any time within the app. If you delete the app, its data is removed from your device.

Changes

This policy may be updated over time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date above. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Contact

If you have questions, feel free to reach out: Email Support
