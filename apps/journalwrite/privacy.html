Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 19, 2025

Offline by Design

JournalWrite does not require an internet connection to function. All data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, analytics, or tracking mechanisms built into the app.

Tip Jar

JournalWrite includes an optional tip jar feature to support development. All payments are processed securely via Apple's in-app purchase system, and we do not collect or store any personal or financial information related to these transactions.

No Ads or In-App Purchases

JournalWrite, aside from the tip jar, is free of advertisements, paywalls, and in-app purchases. Your experience is distraction-free and uninterrupted.

Your Responsibility

Because your data is stored locally, it is your responsibility to back up or secure your journal entries. We do not have access to your content and cannot recover lost data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out at our Support Page.
