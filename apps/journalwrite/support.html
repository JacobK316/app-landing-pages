Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is my journal data stored online?
    A: No. JournalWrite is fully offline. Your entries are stored only on your device and never uploaded or shared.
  • Q: Can I export my writing?
    A: Not yet. All data stays on your device. Export features may be added in future updates.
  • Q: What if I forget my journal lock password?
    A: For your privacy, we don’t store or recover passwords. If you forget it, you may need to reset the app and start fresh.
  • Q: Does JournalWrite collect any personal data?
    A: No. We don’t track, collect, or transmit any personal information. Your journal is yours alone.
