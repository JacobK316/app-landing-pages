Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Does JournalWrite collect any personal data?
    A: No. We don’t track, collect, or transmit any personal information. Your journal data is stored on your device alone.
  • Q: Can I export my writing?
    A: Yes! You can share your journal entries via the share sheet, allowing you to save them to files, email them to yourself, or share them with other apps.
  • Q: Are there any ads or in-app purchases?
    A: No! JournalWrite is completely free of advertisements, paywalls, and in-app purchases. Your experience is distraction-free and uninterrupted.
